Retail Platform

A real shop, sold three ways: an online store, a staffed till and a self-serve kiosk — all running on one codebase against one stock pool, with card payments and Google sign-in.

Visit live ↗

One stock pool, three channels

Web, till and kiosk share the same stock, but each channel is tuned to its own risk: the web reserves stock at the basket, the till trusts the staff member, the kiosk keeps the flow simple. No double-selling, no separate systems to reconcile.

Key features

  • Three sales channels — web shop, staffed till and self-serve kiosk — on one codebase and one stock pool.
  • Reserve-at-basket stock handling on the web channel, so two customers can't buy the last item.
  • Card payments and reader integration via SumUp, covering online and in-person sales.
  • Sign-in with email or Google via django-allauth; UK address lookup via postcodes.io.
  • Product media served from object storage (R2), keeping the little VM light.

How it's built

Back end
